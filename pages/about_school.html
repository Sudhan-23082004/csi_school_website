Our Heritage

School History Timeline

From humble beginnings to educational excellence - trace our journey through the decades

1985

Foundation & Vision

CSI Vellore Diocese School was established with a vision to provide quality Christian education. Started with 50 students and 8 dedicated teachers in a modest building.

1995

Major Expansion

Added new academic blocks, science laboratories, and library. Student enrollment grew to 500+ with introduction of higher secondary classes.

2010

Digital Transformation

Introduced smart classrooms, computer labs, and digital learning platforms. Achieved 100% board exam pass rate for the first time.

2020

Excellence Recognition

Awarded "Best School in Tamil Nadu" by Education Ministry. Successfully adapted to online learning during pandemic with 100% student engagement.

Our Foundation

Mission & Vision

The guiding principles that shape our educational philosophy and commitment to excellence

Our Mission

"To provide holistic education rooted in Christian values, fostering academic excellence, character development, and social responsibility."

We are committed to nurturing each student's intellectual, spiritual, emotional, and physical development through innovative teaching methods, comprehensive curriculum, and a supportive learning environment.

  • Excellence in academic achievement
  • Character formation through Christian values
  • Holistic development of every child

Our Vision

"To be a premier educational institution that develops confident, compassionate, and capable global citizens."

We envision graduates who excel academically while serving their communities with integrity, purpose, and unwavering commitment to making a positive difference in the world.

  • Global citizenship with local values
  • Leadership in service to humanity
  • Innovation in education and life

Leadership Message

A Message from Our Principal

Insights on our educational philosophy and vision for the future

"Education is not just about imparting knowledge; it's about igniting the flame of curiosity, nurturing character, and preparing young minds to face the challenges of tomorrow with confidence and compassion."

Dear Parents and Students, it is with great pride and joy that I welcome you to CSI Vellore Diocese Matriculation Higher Secondary School. For over three decades, our institution has stood as a pillar of educational excellence, rooted in Christian values and committed to holistic development.

Our educational philosophy centers on the belief that every child is uniquely gifted by God. We strive to identify, nurture, and develop these gifts through innovative teaching methodologies, comprehensive curriculum, and a supportive learning environment that encourages both academic achievement and character formation.

In today's rapidly evolving world, we prepare our students not just for examinations, but for life itself. Our focus extends beyond textbooks to include critical thinking, creativity, collaboration, and communication skills that are essential for success in the 21st century.

We believe in the power of partnership between school, parents, and community. Together, we can ensure that our students grow into confident, compassionate, and capable individuals who will make meaningful contributions to society.

Mr. Venugopal V

Principal
