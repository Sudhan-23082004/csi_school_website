Comprehensive Academic Program

Discover our rigorous curriculum designed to nurture intellectual growth, critical thinking, and academic excellence from Kindergarten through Grade 12

Grade Levels

Kindergarten Program

Foundation for lifelong learning

Learning Objectives

  • Develop basic literacy and numeracy skills
  • Foster social and emotional development
  • Encourage creativity through arts and crafts
  • Build motor skills through play-based learning

Key Subjects

Phonics, vocabulary building, storytelling, and basic reading comprehension through interactive activities.
Number recognition, counting, basic shapes, patterns, and simple addition/subtraction concepts.
Nature awareness, seasons, animals, plants, and basic science concepts through observation and exploration.
